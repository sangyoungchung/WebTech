Japan - Ramen Truck

Personally I have visited so many different ramen places (even ichiban ramen in Japan), but if I have to pick one ramen restaurant in Seoul, this ramen truck will not disappoint you. This ramen truck's soup base contain very deep pork bone flavor. Also half-boiled egg and Char Siu for the topping is extraordinary. For professionalism, Ramen Truck only provide ramen as a main menu.
