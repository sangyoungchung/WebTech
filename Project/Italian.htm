Italian - Spacca Napoli

Since Italian cuisine is one of the most popular cuisine in the world, tasting a distinctive Italian is hard to find anywhere. Spacca Napoli restaurant can open your eyes to the new Italian cuisine world. Due to unfamiliarity, Neapolitan pizza was not the first option for pizza lovers. However, Spacca Napoli can be a perfect place to introduce you to Neapolitan pizza. What made this restaurant unique in South Korea is because of unique flavor and sterling qualities. Winner of 2015 world’s Napoli pizza competition run this restaurant. Moreover, this restaurant use traditional Neapolitan Oakwood with Neapolitan certified fire pot. Since Neapolitan food is made with tomatoes and mozzarella cheese, any menu that is tomato sauce based are beyond comparison.
